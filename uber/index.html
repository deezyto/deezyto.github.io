8 причин працювати з нами

No cash, payment occurs automatically
The cost of the trip is paid by the passenger with a linked bank card, i. there are no risks associated with the transportation of cash for both the passenger and the driver, you do not need to look for small change for exchange.
High income with guaranteed payments
We pay wages to the driver's personal bank card! You do not need to register an LLC, sole proprietorship, pay taxes, file a declaration, pay for accounting services and maintain a bank account.
Comfortable, transparent and simple connection
We connect remotely and for free, you don't need to come to us! You send us copies of the documents, we register you and send you connection data, after 1-2 days you can start working!
24/7 support
Uber has 24/7 driver support via e-mail. If something is unclear, you can always ask a question through online consultation.
Commission 8% + BONUS
We have a minimum commission and we pay bonuses for each trip, some of our competitors keep bonuses for themselves. We work honestly and drivers earn more with us!
Work at a convenient time, no schedules
You just press the button to go online to start working, when you decide to finish, click to leave the line. You can detail- tyvat only in the morning, in the evening, on weekends or holidays!
Always a large number of orders
The application shows where there are currently the most orders and where the increased tariffs apply. You will not have downtime and idle runs. Our passengers are always pleasant and absolutely non-conflicting people who are pleasant to drive.
Without checkers and stickers
You do not need to change the color, install "checkers" on the car and cover it with stickers.
